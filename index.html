🏨 Accommodation
Yes — all accommodation for 3rd–6th May is fully covered. You'll receive details in your personalised travel itinerary by mid-April.
Yes. Accommodation and the venue have been arranged through a queer-informed travel agent with longstanding community relationships in Nairobi. GALCK+ has ensured the space is welcoming and safe.
The venue is wheelchair accessible with a quiet/low-stimulation space. Live captioning and dietary accommodations are confirmed. Contact Chantelle for specific needs.
🍽 Food
All meals during the Forum are covered, including the welcome dinner on 4th May. Let Whitney know if your dietary requirements have changed.
Yes. Ensure your dietary needs are in the onboarding form. Contact Whitney if you haven't submitted them yet.

Yes — international flights and accommodation for international participants are fully covered. A participation stipend is also available.
Visa requirements depend on your nationality. If you need a support or invitation letter, request one by 1 April — see the pinned notice on the Notice Board.
